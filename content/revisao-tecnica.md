**Revisão técnica** — technical review — is the work of verifying the technical
accuracy of a book: checking that concepts, terminology, code, and claims hold up
before publication. It's especially common for translated technical and
non-fiction titles.

As an engineer and avid reader, this is work I care about and take on. If you're
an author or publisher who needs a technical reviewer, [get in
touch](mailto:guidopercu@gmail.com).
